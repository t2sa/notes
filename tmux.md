#####TMUX Notes
----

```shell
Ctrl-z                      #Prefix (P)

P-                          #split horizontal
P|                          #split vertical

Pr                          #reload .tmux.conf

Pb                          #brake pane to new window
Pj <window>.<pane>          #join pane from window=1,2.. pane=0,1..
Ps <window>.<pane>          #send pane to window=1,2.. pane=0,1..

PS                          #synchronize on
PU                          #synchronize off
```
